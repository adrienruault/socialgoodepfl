Qeeqbii: an android food assistant
based on barcode scanning of Swiss commodities

Project description

Qeeqbii is an android app that helps users better understand what they eat by scanning the barcode associated to food products. One of the main feature of the app is to warn consumers if their food contains carcinogenic ingredients or if it contains ingredients that they are allergic to. Another important feature is the aggregation of the food that the user have eaten over a period in order to compute statistics about its food consumption. It allows the app to propose nutritional recommendations in order to help the user eating healthier.
The app uses the OpenFood database to retrieve nutritional information concerning Swiss food products.
